The **Overview** page shows what is happening across your server at a glance. Use it to spot activity changes, see where members are most engaged, and understand which channels are carrying the conversation.

  <Card title="Top Channels" icon="fa-solid fa-hashtag">

  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## What you'll see

- **Engagement charts** help you understand how your server's activity changes over time.
- **Metric cards** show the main numbers without making you dig through each channel.
- **Top channel lists** highlight where conversations, reactions, and voice activity are strongest.
- **Date ranges** let you review a day, week, month, year, or custom period when you need more detail.

## How to use it

- Compare server activity before and after announcements, events, or campaigns.
- Look for sudden drops in participation so you can catch possible onboarding or retention issues early.
- Use the busiest channels to decide where automations, social alerts, ticket panels, or moderation tools should go.
- Review custom date ranges after major changes to see whether a spike was temporary or part of a larger trend.

<Callout type="tip">
  After a major event or content push, check this page to see which channels members actually used the most.
</Callout>
