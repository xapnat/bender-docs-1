The **Tickets** section gives your team a structured support workflow with reusable panels, topic-based routing, and searchable transcripts.

## Recommended setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create topics">
    Define the ticket types you want to offer, such as support, reports, billing, or partnerships.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build panels">
    Create the panel messages members will use to open tickets and connect them to the right topics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review settings">
    Set limits, logging, ratings, and transcript behavior before sending panels live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Panels and topics

- **Panels** are the messages members interact with to create a ticket.
- Panel rows can be sent to a channel, updated, edited, duplicated, or deleted.
- The Panel Editor controls the panel name, template embed, ticket buttons, and dropdown menus.
- Dropdown options can define type, label, description, associated topic, optional emoji, and whether the option creates a ticket or opens a form.
- **Topics** define how tickets behave, including name, description, welcome template, naming pattern, open category, closed category, claim category, permissions, and behavior.
- Topic action buttons for Close, Claim, Delete, and Reopen each support label, emoji, and style fields.
- Topics also include support roles, admin roles, and form questions asked when a ticket is created.

## Settings

The settings area controls the day-to-day behavior of your ticket system.

- Set maximum total tickets and maximum per-topic tickets.
- Choose whether counters are global or per topic.
- Choose naming behavior for ticket channels.
- Pick log channels for ticket events and feedback.
- Enable ratings, allow text feedback, and select a rating log channel.
- Decide whether users should receive DMs when tickets are deleted.
- Require a deletion reason when staff delete a ticket.
- Choose templates for deletion logs, deletion DMs, closed, claimed, unclaimed, reopened, inactivity reminder, auto-close, and leave auto-close events.
- Configure member-leave behavior, including whether tickets close or transfer and whether they reopen when a member rejoins.
- Add inactivity rules for time-based reminders or automatic closing.

## Transcripts

Transcripts give staff a record of closed tickets and make it easier to review quality later.

- Search by ticket number or topic.
- Filter by status and sort order.
- Review star ratings and text feedback.
- Open full transcript views when you need the full conversation history.
- Use the empty state as confirmation when no ticket transcripts match your filters.

<Callout type="tip">
  If you care about support quality, enable ticket ratings early. Even a simple star score makes it much easier to spot workflow issues later.
</Callout>
