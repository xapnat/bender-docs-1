## Usage

```
!play <game>
```

## Available Games

- **Blackjack** — Classic card game against the dealer
- **Slots** — Spin the slot machine for big wins
- **Coinflip** — Flip a coin and bet on the outcome
- **Crash** — Cash out before the multiplier crashes
- **Mines** — Reveal tiles without hitting a mine
- **HiLo** — Guess if the next card is higher or lower
- **Towers** — Climb the tower by picking safe tiles
- **Plinko** — Drop a ball and watch it bounce

## Examples

```
!play blackjack 100
!play slots 50
!play coinflip 200 heads
```
